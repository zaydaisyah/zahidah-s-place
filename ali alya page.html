ALYA AND ALI.

This page is about my siblings. As we know, my sister's name is Zahidah Alya and my brother is Zahid Aliyuddin.

I have two amazing siblings who bring so much life to our family. My younger sister, who is 18, and my youngest brother, who is 15, have grown alongside me through all the ups and downs. Despite our differences, we always have each other’s backs, making our relationship stronger with time.
