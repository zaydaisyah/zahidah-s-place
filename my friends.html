HIGH SCHOOL

Friends that I met in boarding school.

High school is a time filled with discovery, challenges, and unforgettable memories, and friends play a significant role in shaping that experience. These are the people who stand by your side as you navigate through exams, extracurricular activities, and personal growth. High school friends are often the ones who share your laughter over the silliest jokes, support you through difficult moments, and cheer you on as you chase your dreams.

Though life may take you on different paths after graduation, high school friends often hold a nostalgic place in your heart. They remind you of a time when life was simpler, yet every moment felt monumental. These friendships teach us the value of loyalty, laughter, and the beauty of shared memories.

UNIVERSITY

Friends in UiTM Rembau.

University friends are truly special, as they walk with you through one of the most transformative phases of your life. These are the people who witness your growth as you step into adulthood, explore your passions, and shape your future. From long nights studying together to celebrating each other’s achievements, university friends become a second family.

Beyond academics, university friends teach you life lessons about trust, compromise, and collaboration. They push you to step out of your comfort zone and remind you to enjoy life along the way. Even after graduation, the memories and connections you make with your university friends remain a priceless part of your journey.

WORKPLACE

Colleagues from my internship.

Internship colleagues hold a special place in your professional journey. They are the people you collaborate with as you take your first steps into the working world, transforming theoretical knowledge into practical skills. These colleagues often become mentors, teammates, and friends, guiding you through unfamiliar tasks and celebrating your progress along the way.

Internship colleagues remind us that the workplace isn’t just about tasks and responsibilities—it’s about the people who support and inspire you to grow and succeed.
