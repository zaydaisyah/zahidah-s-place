GETTING STARTED

IBU AND AYAH.

This page is about my parents. As we know, my dad's name is Mohamed Rasedi and my mom is Nor Azimah.

Parents are the unsung heroes of our lives, always there with unwavering support, love, and guidance. Expressing gratitude to them can sometimes feel challenging, as no words seem enough to convey the depth of our appreciation. Crafting heartfelt thank you messages and quotes for parents is a beautiful way to show your love and acknowledge their sacrifices.

MY MOM

Nor Azimah.

A mother is the ultimate caregiver, always ensuring her family’s needs are met with love and tenderness. She is a source of emotional strength, ready to comfort and support her children through life’s ups and downs. Her nurturing nature and wisdom guide her children to become compassionate and responsible individuals. The sacrifices she makes often go unnoticed, yet they form the foundation of her family’s happiness and success.

  • Attitude:
    1. Unconditional Love: Always shows deep love and care, no matter the situation or challenges faced by her children.
    2. Sacrifice: Willing to put her own needs aside for the well-being and happiness of her family.
    3. Gentle Guidance: Teaches life lessons through care, kindness, and gentle correction, fostering positive values.

MY DAD

Mohamed Rasedi.

A father is the protector and provider, dedicating himself to the safety and well-being of his family. He instills values like hard work, resilience, and integrity in his children, encouraging them to face challenges with confidence. With his quiet strength and unwavering support, he inspires his family to dream big and achieve their goals. His love, though often expressed through actions rather than words, is a constant source of encouragement and stability.

  • Attitude:
    1. Protectiveness: Always ensures the safety and security of his family, physically and emotionally.
    2. Mentorship: Teaches life skills, critical thinking, and responsibility, preparing his children for challenges.
    3. Fairness: Treats everyone in the family with respect and equality, fostering a sense of justice and honesty.
